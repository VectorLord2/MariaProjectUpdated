Let's Go!

Consider the story of Joshua
Victory at Godspeed...

Joshua was the son of Nun, who was Moses’ assistant. He was most known for his work as a military leader and spy for the Israelites, dividing up conquered land for the 12 Tribes.
The story begins when God tells Joshua to go to Canaan, the land He had promised to the Israelites in His covenant with Abraham, and walk every corner of the territory to take authority over the land and lead God’s people to occupy it.
For context, Israel had no weaponry and no army, so Joshua was essentially going into enemy land armed only with God’s promise to him and to Abraham.
Before God sent Joshua into Canaan, He clarified this important promise to him…
